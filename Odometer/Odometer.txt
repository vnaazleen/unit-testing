This odometer can only show digits 1-9 and only the readings that are in ascending order are valid. 
The first valid 4 digit reading is 1234 and the last is 6789. 2467 is valid, while 2314 is not. 
The odometer rolls over to the first valid reading after the last. 

Note: Size of odometer can be of any length Write a set of functions which allow us to carry out the functionalities of the odometer.
